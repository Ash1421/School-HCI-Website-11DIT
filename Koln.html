About Köln

Köln, also known as Cologne in English, is a city with a rich history dating back to the Roman Empire. Today, it is recognized for its economic strength and cultural vibrancy.

Köln is home to many famous landmarks, including the Köln Cathedral, the Roman-Germanic Museum, and the Hohenzollern Bridge. Discover the artistic legacy and thriving arts scene that define the city.

The city is also renowned for its hospitality and diverse culinary offerings, making it a must-visit destination.

One of the most famous bars in Köln is the Brauhaus FRÜH am Dom. The Brauhaus FRÜH am Dom is a traditional German pub located at Am Hof 12-18, 50667 Köln, Germany. When you enter the bar, the waiters might try to sell their own beer and mark it on your cup stand. They draw a wagon wheel with the pen to show how many drinks you have had.

Events

Köln hosts many events throughout the year, including the Köln Carnival, the Köln Christmas Market, and the Köln Summer Festival.

The Köln Carnival is a colorful and lively celebration that takes place in the spring. It features parades, parties, and other festivities. The Köln Christmas Market is a festive market that takes place in the winter. It features a variety of holiday-themed goods and foods, as well as ice skating and other activities. The Köln Summer Festival is a fun and lively celebration that takes place in the summer. It features live music, food, and other activities.
